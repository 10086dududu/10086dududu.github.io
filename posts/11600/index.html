每日总结 7.14

发表于2021-07-14|更新于2021-07-14
|阅读量:

🍜英语

  • experience ①经验，实践 ②经历，阅历 ③经历，体验
    a doctor with experience in dealing with patients suffering from stress在治疗心理应激的病人方面很有经验的医生
    We all learn by experience*.我们都从经验中学习。
    earn from experience 从经验中学习
    In my experience, 据我的观点看，
    from past experience 据过去的经验来看…
    direct/first-hand experience of poverty对贫穷的直接／亲身感受
    Living in Africa was very different from home and quite an experience (= unusualfor us)    .*生活在非洲完全不同于在家里，那真是一次不同寻常的经历。

  • with/without good cause (= with/without a good reason)理由充分；无缘无故

  • a piece of furniture 一件家具

  • Their ambitions were finally dead and buried*.*他们的雄心壮志最终给埋葬了。

  • He buried his face in his hands and wept.他双手掩面而泣。（掩盖，遮掩）（其它意思有：埋葬；覆盖（被动））

  • elegant ladies in furs 穿着裘皮衣服的高雅贵妇

  • Sugar occurs naturally in fruit*.*水果天然含糖分。（occur:①发生 ②存在于）

  • He met with a violent death (= he was murdered, killed in a fight, etc.).他遭暴力致死。
    violent crime 暴力犯罪
    The crowd suddenly turned violent    .人群中突然有人开始大打出手。
    There was a violent reaction from the public    .公众的反应强烈。
    I took a violent dislike to him    .*我很讨厌他。（猛烈的，强烈的）

  • Huge waves were breaking on the shore*.*巨浪拍打着海岸。
    ride the waves 冲浪
    in/on the waves 在里面/在表面
    a wave of opposition/protest/violence, etc.反对、抗议、暴力等的浪潮
    a crime wave犯罪潮（汹涌的行动（或思想）态势；心潮；风潮）
    Wave after wave of aircraft passed overhead*.一批又一批飞机从上空掠过。（涌现的人（或事物）；涌动的人（或物））
    He gave us a wave as the bus drove off    .*公共汽车开走时他向我们挥了挥手。（挥手）
    radio/sound/ultrasonic waves无线电波；声波；超声波（热声光的波；波状运动）

  • She struck him in the face.她掴了他一记耳光。
    Who struck the first blow (= started the fight)*?是谁先动手的？
    Police fear that the killer may strike again    .警方担心杀人犯可能再次下手 （袭击；攻击）
    Two days later tragedy struck    .*两天后悲剧发生了。 （疾病，灾难 侵蚀，爆发）
    An awful thought has just struck me.刚才我脑子里突然闪过一个可怕的念头。（突然想到；一下子想起；猛地意识到）
    It suddenly struck me how we could improve the situation*.*我一下子明白我们如何能改善局面了。

    His reaction struck me as odd*.他的反应令我诧异。
    How does the idea strike you    ?*你觉得这个主意怎么样？
    She strikes me as a very efficient person.在我眼里，她是个很干练的人。
    it strikes sb that…It strikes me that nobody is really in favour of the changes.我觉得没人真正赞成这些变动。
